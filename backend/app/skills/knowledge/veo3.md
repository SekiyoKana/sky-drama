## Veo 3 Prompt Template: Professional Filmmaking
**Prompt:**
Cinematic wide shot, establishing shot. A lone astronaut stands on the edge of a red cliff on Mars, looking out at a giant blue Earth rising on the horizon. Dust storms swirl in the valley below.
**Lighting:** High-contrast sunset lighting, long shadows.
**Style:** IMAX 70mm film aesthetic, grain, high resolution, photorealistic.
**Camera:** Slow dolly zoom (vertigo effect) creating a sense of isolation.

**Breakdown (Why it works):**
- **Shot Syntax:** Starts immediately with "Cinematic wide shot" (Veo prioritizes the first tokens for composition).
- **Technical Specs:** "IMAX 70mm", "Dolly zoom" triggers specific visual effects Veo is fine-tuned on.
- **Structure:** Separating Lighting/Style/Camera helps Veo parse complex instructions.
